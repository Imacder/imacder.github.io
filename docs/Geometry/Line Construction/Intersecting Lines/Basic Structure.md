$$ \triangle OAB \sim \triangle OCD $$

There are actually 2 sub-cases:

1. $AB$ and $CD$ are on the same side. (A-type)
2. $AB$ and $CD$ are on opposite sides of $O$. (X-type)

## Properties

1. $ OA \cdot OD = OB \cdot OC $
2. $ABCD$ are concyclic &rArr; other [similar triangles](../../Circles/Similar%20Triangles%20in%20Circle.md#basic-similarities)
3. $ \frac{AB}{CD} = \frac{OA}{OC} = \frac{OB}{OD} $

## Usage

1. Collinear products
2. Concyclic quadrilaterals
