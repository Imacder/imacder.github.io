# Linear Algebra

## How to Derive Tranformation Matricies

1. Start out with the basis vectors $\hat{\mathbf{x}} ,\, \hat{\mathbf{y}}$ </br>
2. Find their transformed results: $\hat{\mathbf{x}} \rightarrow \mathbf{u} ,\, \hat{\mathbf{y}} \rightarrow \mathbf{v}$
3. Put them together:

$$ \begin{bmatrix} 
-\mathbf{u}- \\ 
-\mathbf{v}-
\end{bmatrix} $$
